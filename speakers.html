The Speakers

Dr. Pai Zheng

Pai Zheng is currently an Associate Professor, Wong Tit Shing Endowed Young Scholar in Smart Robotics in the Department of Industrial and Systems Engineering, and Director of Human-Machine Symbiotic Design and Manufacturing Laboratory (HMS-DML) Platform, at the Hong Kong Polytechnic University, HKSAR. Dr. Zheng is a recipient of the HKIE Young Engineer of the Year Award (2025), SME Outstanding Young Manufacturing Engineers Award (2024), NSFC Excellent Young Scientists Fund (2024), HKPU Young Innovative Researcher Award (2023), and Top 50 Global “AI+X” Chinese Young Scholars (2022). His research interest mainly lies in human-robot collaboration, smart product-service systems, and industrial AI. He is a Senior Member of IEEE (2022–)/CMES (2018–), Member of SME (2022–)/ASME (2017–), CIRP Associate Member (2024–), SME | NAMRC Scientific Committee Member of Track 1: Manufacturing Systems, Technical Committee Co-chair of IEEE Robotics and Automation Society TC-Digital Manufacturing and Human-Centred Automation, and CMES Industrial Big Data and Intelligent Systems Chapter. He also serves as the Associate Editor of Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing, IEEE Transactions on Automation Science and Engineering, SME Journal of Manufacturing Systems, etc.

Dr. Cheng Liu

Cheng Liu received the B.S. degree in aerospace engineering from Nanjing University of Aeronautics and Astronautics, Nanjing, China, in 2013, and the M.S. degree in aeronautics and astronautics and the Ph.D. degree in mechanical engineering from Stanford University, Stanford, CA, USA, in 2015 and 2021, respectively. He is currently an Assistant Professor in the Department of Systems Engineering, City University of Hong Kong. His current research interests include Physics-Guided Machine Learning for Robotic Tactile Sensing, Smart Structures, Cyber-physical Systems/Digital Twin, Structural Health Monitoring (SHM) and Mechanics of Composite Structures.

Dr. Yiwei Wang

Yiwei Wang is an associated Professor in School of Mechanical Engineering and Automation, Beihang University and won the Beihang's top young talents award. Her research interests include industrial intelligence, mixed reality wearable devices-aided intelligent assembly, human-robot collaborative assembly, knowledge-based and data-driven fault diagnostics, prognostics and health management. She has several years of industry intelligence methods R&D experiences on assembly and machinery operation and maintenance in the industry. She hosted and participated in 20 projects funded by the National Natural Science Foundation, the Ministry of Industry and Information Technology (MIIT) Major Science and Technology, etc. She has published more than 30 papers in high-level SCI journals and international conferences, and won Best Paper Awards in multiple international conferences. She has published two books regarding intelligent manufacturing. She has ten invention patents and software copyrights, and participated in the formulation of the National Standard GB/T41272-2022. She serves as a standing committee member of two societies including Smart Factory Professional Committee of Graphics Society and Industrial Big Data and Intelligent Systems committee of Mechanical Engineering Society.

Dr. Peng (Edward) Wang

Peng (Edward) Wang is currently an Associate Professor in the Department of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering at Case Western Reserve University (CWRU). Dr. Wang has extensive experience in developing novel ML methodologies for machine condition monitoring and diagnosis, process modeling and quality prediction, and collaborative robots. Dr. Wang is the recipient of the CAREER award from the US National Science Foundation in 2023, Young Investigator Award from the International Symposium of Flexible Automation in 2024, Outstanding Young Manufacturing Engineer Award from the Society of Manufacturing Engineers (SME) in 2022, the Best Paper Award from the 2023 Manufacturing Science and Research Conference (MSEC), Outstanding Technical Paper Award from the SME North American Manufacturing Research Conference (NAMRC) in 2017, 2020, and 2021, and other best paper awards. Dr. Wang is an Associate Editor of the IEEE Sensors Journal and Journal of Intelligent Manufacturing.

Dr. Loris Roveda

Loris Roveda is an associate professor at Politecnico di Milano (Italy) and senior researcher at IDSIA SUPSI (Switzerland) working in the field of robotics and machine learning. He graduated with MSc degree in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano in 2011. He obtained his PhD in Mechanical Engineering in Mechanical Engineering at Politecnico di Milano in 2015. His main interests are related to generative AI for robotics, multi-modal human-robot interaction, task learning and generalization, and exoskeleton design and control. He is the author of international papers and coordinator of international and national projects.

Dr. Domenico Campolo

Domenico Campolo is currently Professor at the School of Mechanical and Aerospace Engineering and member of Robotics Research Centre, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore. He received his Laurea Degree (1998) in Electronics Engineering from the University of Pisa, Italy, his Diploma Degree (1999) in Engineering and his PhD (2002) in Micro-Engineering from Scuola Superiore Sant’Anna, Pisa. In 2000–2003, he was at UC Berkeley (USA). In 2003–2009, he worked at Campus Bio-Medico University in Rome (Italy) and, since 2009, he has been with NTU. He is co-founder of ArtiCares Pte Ltd, an NTU spinoff company focusing on Robotic Technologies for decentralized healthcare, including homes. His research interests include human-machine physical interaction and motor control in humans and robots with applications to industry & healthcare.
