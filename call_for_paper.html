To encourage participation, we plan to advertise the workshop in the following ways:

1. Social Media Platforms:

  • Utilize platforms such as Twitter, LinkedIn, and Facebook to share workshop details widely.
  • Promote through WeChat (e.g., Official Accounts, Groups, Moments) to reach audiences in the Chinese-speaking community.

Mailing Lists:

  • Disseminate information via robotics-related mailing lists (e.g., RAS Technical Committees).
  • Utilize mailing lists from university, the speakers’ institutions, and collaborating organizations.

Conferences and Events:

  • Promote the workshop at key events and conferences where we are presenting (e.g., IEEE International Conference on Automation Science and Engineering (CASE), Frontiers of Digital Twins in Intelligent Manufacturing and Smart Cities).
