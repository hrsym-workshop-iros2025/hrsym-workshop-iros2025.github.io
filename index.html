October 20th 2025, Workshop on


Human-Robot Symbiosis Towards Manufacturing Metaverse: Opportunities and Challenges


at 2025 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems

Hangzhou, CHINA

Objectives

Manufacturing Metaverse plays a pivotal role in creating an expansive digital manufacturing environment that frees humans from the limitations of body, mind, space, and time, significantly increasing the scale and efficiency of production. Especially as robots continue to advance in intelligence—prevailing cooperation, interaction, and collaboration with humans—a human-robot symbiosis paradigm toward the manufacturing Metaverse (HRSyM) is emerging to balance productivity improvements with the enrichment of human lifestyles, sparking mixed reactions and discussions.

This workshop centers on the foreseeable HRSyM—an immersive symbiotic system where human and robots maximize their complementary abilities for flexible and efficient production in both shared physical workspaces and remote virtual environments. Through interactive talks and panel discussions, we aim to bring together researchers from interdisciplinary fields to discuss key opportunities and challenges for the implementation of HRSyM. By bridging theory and practice, we seek solutions that enable high-fidelity 3D construction of fused physical-virtual environments, industrial scene spatial intelligence representation, human-robot augmented operation skills throughout the production lifecycle, and physics-based intelligence for dexterous manipulation, while addressing practical applications like assembly, welding, grinding, polishing, and maintenance in high-end manufacturing and Industry 4.0/5.0.

Aligned with the IROS 2025 theme, "Human-Robotics Frontier," this event aims to foster interdisciplinary collaboration, accelerate the development of sustainable, resilient, and human-centric industrial systems and reshape the entire lifecycle of products—from design and production to delivery, usage, and maintenance.

Topics of Interest

Given this background and motivation, topics of interest include but are not limited to:

  • Human-robot symbiotic manufacturing Metaverse framework
  • Human-robotic avatar modelling in manufacturing
  • Virtual-physical fused human-robot interaction
  • Human-robot roaming and navigation in Metaverse
  • Open-vocabulary robot operation skills in physical-digital settings
  • Contact-rich, dexterous robot manipulation for tactile intelligence
  • Multi-physics simulation for manufacturing Metaverse
  • High-fidelity 3D reconstruction of human-robot-environment elements
  • Human-AI augmented intelligence for human-robot decision making and task planning
  • Foundation models in manufacturing Metaverse
  • Low-code platform for seamless robot skill scaling
  • Self-organizing multiple human-robot collaboration
  • Multimodal perception through visual, auditory, physiological, or tactile senses
  • Brain robotics, including brainwave pattern recognition and robot control
  • Knowledge representation in task planning and role allocation
  • Human-robot symbiosis solutions in manufacturing
